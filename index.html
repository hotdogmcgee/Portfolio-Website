Your Brake Light is Out - Full Stack Developer

Help save lives and prevent accidents by ensuring everyone's brake lights work.

A web app that I developed solo for a client, released in 2022. A key aspect of YBLIO is protecting user data. There will be more to build, it is currently closer to an MVP state.

Users are guided through a form wizard to either set up a notification for their own plate or report another. The goal is to know as soon as possible if a brake, tail, or head light is out.

Music Book Reviews

A fullstack application for students, parents, and educators to find and review educational music books. The intention of the app is to use detailed input from educators to make clear what a given book contains and how it is or is not valuable. I have often dealt with the frustration of publisher-supplied information being the only source to use when assessing a book and wanted a way for everyone involved in music education to aggregate a knowledge base in a focused manner.
